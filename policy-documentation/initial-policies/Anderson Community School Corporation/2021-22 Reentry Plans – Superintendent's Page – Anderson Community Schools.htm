2021-22 Reentry Plans

The Anderson Community School Corporation (ACSC) School Board approved updated COVID-19 procedures at their July 13, 2021 meeting. Key updates for the 2021-2022 school year include:

  • Masks are REQUIRED* for all students (PK-12) and staff.
  • Masks are REQUIRED on school buses and school transportation. This is a federal mandate. We will provide masks for schools and buses.
  • School days return to the normal schedule.
  • Return to the normal 5 days per week schedule:
    • Elementary: 9:00 am - 3:30 pm
    • Secondary: 7:30 am - 2:30 pm
 
*This goes into effect on Monday, August 9th and will last until Friday, August 20th.
