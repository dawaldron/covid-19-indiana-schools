                                  MSD of New Durham Township Return to Learn 2021-2022


                                  The Board of School Trustees approved the Return to Learn 2021-2022 Plan on 7/22/21.  Our plan will include a color-coded system whereby each color dictates the level of precautionary measures to be implemented throughout the school.  Please be aware that administration will gather data on a daily basis and the school's current level could change at any time based on changes in risk level.

                                  YELLOW

                                  Large group meetings/assemblies not permitted.

                                  *Risk of transmission will be determined by school administration in collaboration with local health officials, and will take into account active school cases as well as close contact quarantine cases.

                                  **Westville’s Blackhawk Academy is a K-12 fully virtual alternative learning program that is available to a select number of students based on qualifying medical need or other circumstances.  Interested students must apply prior to the start of a semester and be approved by administration.  Contact your school principal for further information.
