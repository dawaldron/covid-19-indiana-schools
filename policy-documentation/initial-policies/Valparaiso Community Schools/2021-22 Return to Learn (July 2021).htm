Plan Overview 2021-22

This multi-tiered approach was designed to outline the structure and procedures in place for the 2021-22 school year. Level of incidence and subsequent protocols will be based on the Indiana Department of Health dashboard and/or the Porter County Health Department COVID Advisory Level.
