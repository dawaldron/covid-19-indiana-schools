COVID-19 PROTOCOLS

Masks/Face Coverings

According to CDC guidelines, masks are recommended indoors for all individuals, vaccinated and unvaccinated. In Avon Schools, masks are recommended for all individuals (but not required).

Masks are required on buses by federal mandate.

At any time, under the direction of the Hendricks County Health Department, masks may be required at a classroom, school, or district level.

Avon Schools will follow contact tracing and quarantine guidelines for those who have been in close contact with anyone who tests positive for COVID-19 in schools.

'Close contact' is defined as being within six feet of a COVID+ individual for 15 minutes since masks are not required.

Students deemed to be a close contact may be exempted from quarantine if proof of vaccination is shared with the school nurse.

PreK-6: Cafeterias will spread out to maximize distance and group students in pods for contact tracing.

7-12: Cafeterias will return to normal with seating charts.

No outside food deliveries or lunch visitors.

Masks and face coverings are required on school buses regardless of vaccination status.

This protocol is in compliance with the federal government's public transportation mandate.

COVID-19 LESSONS

Hand Washing

Proper hand washing and sanitizing limits the spread of COVID-19.

Vaccination

Being vaccinated is one way to protect oneself and others from COVID-19.

Safe at Home

Monitoring health and remaining home when symptoms appear is vital.

COVID-19 Testing

Testing is reliable and sites are conveniently located in the community.

Divided Clinics

Using separate areas for students with symptoms protects healthy students.

Social Distancing

Using seating charts and consistent student groups inhibits the spread.

After 18 months of in-person education during the pandemic, we know these practices will serve us well this year.

These protocols were developed in consultation with our local Department of Health and are subject to change.

Before- and after-school student activities can return to normal under updated mask and quarantine policies.

Students must attend school in-person to participate.

Plexiglass barriers will remain installed in front offices.

Masks are recommended for all guests to all ACSC facilities.

Please refrain from entering schools or facilities if you have symptoms of COVID-19. (See the next page for a list of symptoms.)

Volunteers who complete ACSC's volunteer application process are permitted in schools during the school day.
