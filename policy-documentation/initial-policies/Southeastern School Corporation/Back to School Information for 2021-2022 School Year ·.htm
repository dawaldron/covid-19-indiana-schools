Back to School Information for 2021-2022 School Year

August 1, 2021
Cass County Online

Last Updated on August 1, 2021 by Cass County Online

(This info was originally posted on July 20, 2021)

As schools in Cass County, Indiana prepare for the 2021-2022 school year, we thought it would be helpful to gather some links in one post.

There may be updates so continue to watch for additional information from your school. We are providing these links for your convenience. If you have specific questions, please check with the school corporation.

Lewis Cass Schools
First Student Day: Friday, August 6, 2021
School Calendar (PDF)

School start date for all students is August 6. First teacher day is August 4 and new teacher orientation is August 2.

The Elementary and the Jr./Sr. High School will have an open house on August 5 from 5-7pm. Please bring your school supplies.

The Lewis Cass School Board reviewed and approved the Lewis Cass Return To School Plan that details the guidelines and expectations for students, staff and parents when returning to school. To ensure continuity of operations of essential functions and for the safety of our staff, students and community the plan will allow Lewis Cass Schools to continue to stay open at 100% capacity for in school instruction. The Lewis Cass Return to School Plan will also be posted on the Corporation website and we will update the status of the school as the state and local health departments change status. Item three on the return to school plan states: Lewis Cass will follow Federal, CDC, State and local health guidelines/mandates/executive orders for masks. As the state and local health departments change status we will update students, staff and parents and post the changes on our Lewis Cass website. Our current status as of July 22, 2021, masks are optional in all school buildings and masks are to be worn on a school bus. The wearing of masks on a bus is a federal mandate for public transportation. Please visit our website for a full description of the return to school plan.

Corporation Website: www.lewiscass.net
