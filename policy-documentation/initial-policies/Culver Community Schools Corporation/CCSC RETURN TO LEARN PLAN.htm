Culver Community Schools


Back to School 2021 - 2022

CCSC has been working with our local health departments and other area school superintendents to design a plan to return as safely as possible to school on August 9, 2021.

Virtual Learning Platform will be considered on an Individual Student Basis with a conference with parents and administrators before allowing student to be virtual during anytime for the 2021-22 school year.

TO MAKE OUR RETURN HAPPEN AND TO PREVENT THE FURTHER SPREAD:

Wearing a face covering when social distancing cannot occur is recommended for individuals who wish to reduce their risk of infection or is required by the school board/health department.

Wash your hands often.

Monitor your daily temperature and symptoms daily.

If you suspect you have COVID-19, please be tested to limit the spread.

OUR GOAL: ALL STUDENTS PHYSICALLY PRESENT IN SCHOOL.

Students are required to provide their own mask.

We are improving our HVAC systems in both buildings that will improve the air quality in the buildings allowing more fresh air in and circulate air through filters that are designed to improve air quality.

We have also replaced exterior doors which allows in the HVAC system to be most efficient.

We have changed flooring in some classrooms and areas to improve air quality.


TO ACHIEVE OUR GOAL:

We are taking the steps possible to increase the space in our classrooms, cafeteria, and hallways so that social distancing of 3-6 feet can be obtained.

We have moved Culver Boys and Girls Club to the Middle School to allow for more space at the elementary and provide them their own area to keep sanitized.

WE NEED PARENT ASSISTANCE ON OUR BUSSES. IF YOU CAN TRANSPORT YOUR CHILD TO SCHOOL, PLEASE LET US KNOW BY CLICKING HERE

We are in the process of designing different areas for pick-up and drop-off to reduce congestion in hallways and increase social distancing in common areas.
