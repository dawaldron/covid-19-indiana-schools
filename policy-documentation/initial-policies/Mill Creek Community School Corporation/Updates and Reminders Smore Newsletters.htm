Updates and Reminders

July 15, 2021

Mill Creek Athletic Excellence 4

A Few Highlights!

1. Summer time 2021 continues! Enjoy the time together with family and friends.


2. We look forward to ALL of our students returning for IN PERSON INSTRUCTION for the 2021-22 school year. Online registration for the 2021-22 school year continues. Please complete in a timely fashion. Registration will need to be complete prior to Chromebook distribution on July 26 & 27. Please click on the button below for an "Infographic" for picking up Chromebooks.


  • CMS cafeteria outside door #14, drive thru pick up
  • July 26, 11:00 am - 7:00 pm
  • July 27, 7:00 am - 12:00 pm and 4:30 pm - 6:30 pm



3. We have received guidance from the HC Medical Officer and Department for the 2021-22 school year. Our Return to Instruction protocols were approved by school board last night, July 14, as recommended! Please click on the button below to review. The major changes for this school year:


  • Masks are optional (recommended).
  • Contact tracing notifications.
  • Vaccinated individuals (guests, visitors, and volunteers) may enter our schools during the school day by following procedures.


4. Please click on the buttons below for additional information. We aim to keep you informed!

  • Return to Instruction Protocols for the 2021-22 school year
  • School Board Summary, July 14, 2021
  • Seamless Summer Option - Child Nutrition Program
  • Online Registration for the 2021-22 School Year
  • Chromebook Distribution "Infographic"
  • Employment opportunities. Please consider serving our school district by joining our team as a substitute teacher, bus driver, or custodian. We need YOU!
  • COVID-19 Vaccinations - Everyone age 12+ is highly encouraged to get the vaccine.
  • 21-22 Indiana Required and Recommended Vaccinations
  • 2021-22 Little Cadets Preschool Information
  • Quick Links to Maintain Communication!
  • Food Service Updates - FREE breakfast and lunch meals for ALL Students through July 30, 2022!
  • 2021-22 Academic Calendar - Revised Approved 6/9/21
  • 2022-23 Academic Calendar - Approved 6/9/21


5. The MCCSC School Board meetings will be held at the Administrative Services Center, 6631 S. CR 200 W, Clayton, IN 46118. The monthly meetings will be held on the second Wednesday of each month beginning at 6:00 pm.
