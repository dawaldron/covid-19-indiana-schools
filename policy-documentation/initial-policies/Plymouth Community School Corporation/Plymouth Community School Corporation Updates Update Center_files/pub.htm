More Information about P-EBT Funds:

Indiana was recently approved for the COVID response program called Pandemic EBT (Electronic Benefit Transfer). All Indiana students who were participating in the Free & Reduced Lunch benefit should be receiving benefits either to their household’s existing SNAP EBT card, or a new card issued to the household for just this benefit if the family was not on SNAP. More information about the program can be found here: https://frac.org/pebt

 

You can also see the flyer at the top of this page.

 

Some things to note about this program:

 

  • Using P-EBT benefits does not impact you or your child’s immigration status. The public charge rule does not apply to P-EBT benefits. These benefits replace school meals and are for all families that receive free and reduced-price school meals.
  • P-EBT benefits supplement grab and go student meal sites. They do not replace them.
  • Benefits can only be used to purchase SNAP eligible items, more information here: https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/eligible-food-items
